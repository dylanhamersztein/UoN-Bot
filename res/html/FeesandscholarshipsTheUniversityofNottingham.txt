School of Computer Science Postgraduate Taught Scholarships Please note that for degrees awarded overseas, the degree classification must be equivalent to the appropriate UK classification, and in some cases there is not a direct match.
No application form is required for these School postgraduate taught scholarships.
Cheques will be automatically generated and issued to successful students at the appropriate time.
